IIT KHARAGPUR:THE DREAMLAND OF AN ENGINEER

This is IIT Kharagpur. It is a paradise for any engineer. This is the place where you can fulfill all your dreams. IIT Kharagpur which has maximum number of branches among all IITs, not only provides great scope in academics but also helps a student excel in many of his/her interests.This IIT helps a student find his skills in various fields. Here are the some of the major events in IIT KHARAGPUR.

Kshitij is the largest techno-management fest in Asia attracts great talent from all over India.

Spring Fest is one of the best socio-cultural fest held in India in which some of the greatest artists set the floor.

Illumination is one of the most unique events held by IIT KGP in Diwali celebrating the true essence of Diwali by lighting only Diyas,not Crackers.

Rangoli held in IIT KGP is a great event held in Diwali
