IIT KHARAGPUR

This is the place where I study. Truly, speaking after the JEE counselling I felt like I have fallen in in the wrong place. I thought this IIT is not good enough and like others I had that fantasy for IIT-Bombay and IIT-Delhi. After arriving here, I felt god has added more added more to my misery as I came to know that I ended up in this oldest IIT.


But now my feelings have taken a huge turn. I feel I am in the best college of this country and I am lucky enough to have got RK Hall in first year,undoubtedly the best hall in KGP. The best in this hall is that we have great interaction with our batchmates as well as seniors. Really feel lucky to have ended up here.


I have introduced this IIT a bit in MY GALLERY. Let me tell you ABOUT ME this IIT more.

This is BC ROY HOSPITAL which gives 24*7 free medical service to students.


This is Tech-Market, which contains all types of shops for easy access for students.


This is the Nalanda Class Complex where all the theory classes of students are held. All the rooms are air-conditioned with great projectors.


This is Technology Students Gymkhana(TSG) which controls all the sports and games and all other extra-curricular students for the students. Under the TSG,comes all the "Societies".


I have shown some of the glimpses of this IIT. I will advise you to visit this Engineer's Paradise for sure
