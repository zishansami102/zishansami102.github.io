FOOTBALL

Football forms an integral part of my life. I love it from it bottom of my heart.

My journey started with football in an very awkward way. I did not watch football till 2012.

One friend joined my school in that year and that year there was the Champions League Final between FC Barcelona and Man Utd. He supported Barca and I went with Man Utd without knowing much(Yes,Barca was stronger).That match Man Utd lost and that's where it all began. I decided come what may hem. I will never leave Man Utd and since then I have been a RED DEVIL

From then my journey with Football has been epic. I have had everything. I saw Man Utd win the PL next season. Then the long trophy drought. That period was really very painful.But now Jose Mourinho has brought us back. What I love ABOUT ME Manchester United is that their bounce-back ability.That is what sets them apart from others.

From how much I have watched football now there are three mainruling teams:FC Barcelona,Real Madrid CF, FC Bayern Munich. They are pretty much the dominant force. But PSG, Chelsea, Man United, Man City and Juventus are slowly entering the elite group.

For me the best international side is Germany and Netherlands are always the chokers.

My favourite goal is the bicycle kick of Rooney for Man Utd against Man City

My favourite player is Messi and my favourite young player is Anthony Martial
