MUSIC

I used to think listening to song as bad when I was a child. But it started when I went to Kota for first time. I heard first Linkin Park. I loved it. Then, I started listening to songs. I started enjoying. Then somehow I found Imagine Dragons(Radioactive). I felt like wow. Then my real journey began with songs.I love pop the most. I should say somehow I discovered Imagine Dragons. Imagine Dragons are my favourite.What sets them apart is that they compose great songs with superb lyrics and superb music.Normally they take up pop as their genre.

Ed Sheeran is also like them with both great lyrics and great music. He has also written for many other bands and singers. One Republic are also a great band and I love their "I Lived" the most.The Script are also one of the best.Who doesn't like their "Hall ofFame". Next Bieber, Adam Levine and Adam Lambert are also really great. One Direction are also marvellous. Fall Out Boys are no behind.Lastly,the evergreen: Backstreet Boys.

I also like old songs. Mohammed Rafi is unmatchable.Kishore Kumar is one of the best. Lata Mangeshkar has goddess Saraswati on her throat. Who doesn't like her!!!!

As I am an Odia, I also love the bhajans of Lord Jagannath. The songs of Bhikari Bal and Namita Agarwal are the ones which fill my spiritual diet.

Here are few recommendations for you.

  • "I Lived" by One Republic
  • "Radioactive" by Imagine Dragons
  • "Rise Up" by Imagine Dragons
  • "Walking the Wire" by Imagine Dragons
  • "Hall of Fame" by The Script
  • "Champions" by Fall Out Boy
  • "Love Me Like You Do" by Ellie Goulding
  • "Confident" by Demi Lovato
  • "Perfect" by One Direction
  • "Permanent Stain" by Backstreet Boys
  • "Show em what You Are Made Are Of" by Backstreet Boys
